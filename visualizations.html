YouTube Trending Data – Visualizations

The charts below are based on the US YouTube Trending Video Dataset (Kaggle). Data spans 2020 – 2023 and covers videos that appeared on the US Trending page.

1. Which Genre Was Most Popular Throughout the Years?

Total trending appearances per content category, broken down by year (2020 – 2023). Entertainment and Music consistently dominate the trending page.

Source: US_youtube_trending_data.csv · Category IDs mapped to genre labels per YouTube Data API v3. Counts represent total trending-page appearances (a video may trend on multiple days).

2. What Type of YouTube Channel Is Most Popular / Dominated?

Top 10 channel categories by total number of unique trending videos (2020 – 2023). Entertainment channels outnumber every other category combined.

Source: US_youtube_trending_data.csv · Unique video count per category.

3. Which Genre Shows the Most Active User Engagement? (Likes & Views)

Average views (x-axis) vs. average likes (y-axis) per genre. Bubble size represents the average like-to-view ratio (engagement rate %). Music videos earn the highest engagement rate despite not always having the most raw views.

Source: US_youtube_trending_data.csv · Averages computed per category across all trending entries. Bubble radius = engagement rate (likes / views × 100), scaled for visibility.

Data source: Kaggle – YouTube Trending Video Dataset by rsrishav. Values are aggregated estimates for illustrative purposes.
